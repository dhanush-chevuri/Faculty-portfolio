RESEARCH PROJECTS

B.E. Project

Title: Experimental study on poly propylene fiber reinforced self-compacting concrete.
Description: Finding the compressive and tensile strength of the concrete when added with self-compacting materials and poly propylene fibers.

Ph.D. Thesis

Title: Prediction of Energy Demand and Availability Towards Strategic Planning of Energy Resources
Description: The study's objective is to statistically analyze and estimate the exceedance probability of the energy requirement and availability. It proposes a model for long-term forecasting of energy and water requirements, and a model for long-term energy and water availability forecast.

M.Tech. Thesis

Title: Water and Wastewater Treatment by using Natural Materials
Description: The objective of the study is to analyze and investigate the efficiency by which turbidity, TDS, colour, COD and microbial load can be removed by using natural materials such as coagulant, disinfectant and others.
